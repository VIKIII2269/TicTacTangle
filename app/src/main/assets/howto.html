How to Play

Objective

The objective of this game is to win three individual Tic Tac Toe boards in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Game Setup

It consists of a 3x3 grid, where each cell contains a smaller Tic Tac Toe board. Players take turns making a move in one of the smaller boards.

Rules

  • Players take turns making a move in one of the smaller Tic Tac Toe boards.
  • The board in which the next player must play is determined by the opponent's previous move.
  • To win a smaller board, a player must win three cells in a row, just like traditional Tic Tac Toe.
  • The first player to win three smaller boards in a row wins the game.
